Enjoy Seasonal Japanese Cuisine at Kukai

Kukai has three branches in Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture. At our restaurants that offer traditional Japanese kaiseki cuisine, you can enjoy delicious, delicate, and beautiful Japanese meals that use seasonal ingredients. We have large and small private rooms at each of our restaurants, so you can relax and enjoy your meal in a peaceful environment, which is another charm of our restaurants. We hope that the time you spend at one of our restaurants will become a superb memory as part of your travels in Japan.

The Spirit of Harmony in the Cuisine We Prepare

The island country of Japan, surrounded by seas on all sides, has an abundance of ingredients that change with the seasons.
Our chefs showcase their skills in their ability to create truly delicious cuisine using these seasonal ingredients.
While they naturally make use of their accumulated creative originality, what is more crucial is that they think of every meal they cook as one they are serving to their family or people dear to them.
This is how they are able to serve cuisine with a spirit of hospitality.
